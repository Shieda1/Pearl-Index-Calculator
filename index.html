What is the pearl index calculator?
The pearl index calculator is a tool that evaluates the difference between contraceptions, drugs and methods that prevent you from getting pregnant.
How to calculate pearl index?
To calculate pearl index you should use the following formula:
pearl index = (number of pregnancies * 12) * 100 / (number of women in the study * study duration)
Why use pearl index calculator?
The pearl index calculator is used in order to evaluate the difference between contraceptions, drugs and methods that prevent you from getting pregnant.
